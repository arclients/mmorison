Let's Talk About Your Insurance

At, Stewart Morrison Insurance Brokers Ltd, we value your business, because you’re not simply another customer to us, you’re our client. When you contact us, you will speak personally to a licensed Insurance Broker who will provide a thorough evaluation of your personal or business insurance needs. Stewart Morrison Insurance Brokers Ltd, partners with the leading insurance companies in Canada to allow us the ability to offer competitive pricing and the best coverage options. Our qualified team of insurance specialists will make recommendations for the right coverage to properly protect you in the event of a claim. We are a family owned insurance brokerage having been in operation since 1971, with our offices conveniently located throughout the Kawarthas and Durham Region. Our corporate head office is located in Lindsay, Ontario with branch offices in the communities of Port Perry, Fenelon Falls, Peterborough, Woodville and Bobcaygeon to serve you.
